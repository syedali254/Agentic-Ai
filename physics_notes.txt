
Q1: What is gravity?
A1: Gravity is a moving object in opposite direction and attracting each other

Q2: State Newton's First Law of Motion.
A2: An object stays still or moves in a straight line unless something makes it stop or change direction.

Q3: What is the First Law of Thermodynamics?
A3: It says energy can be created or destroyed but it can not change from one form to another.
